## Data We Collect
When you register, order, or interact with our services, we may collect:
- **Account information** (name, email address, username)  
- **Billing details** (payment method, transaction records)  
- **Server data** (IP address, usage logs, configuration files)  
- **Support interactions** (tickets, live chat messages)  

We only collect information that is necessary to deliver and improve your experience.

---

## How We Use Your Data
Your data is used for:
- Account creation and authentication  
- Billing, payment, and service activation  
- System monitoring and performance improvement  
- Fraud detection and abuse prevention  
- Customer support and service notifications  

LokaNode does **not sell or trade** your personal data to third parties.

---

## Cookies & Tracking
Our website may use cookies and analytics tools to enhance usability and gather anonymous statistics such as:
- Page views, time spent, and navigation behavior  
- Error logs and loading performance  

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work as intended.

---

## Data Protection
We take your security seriously.  
All sensitive data — including passwords and billing details — is **encrypted in transit (SSL)** and **stored securely** with limited access.  

Regular audits, access logs, and automated monitoring are implemented to prevent unauthorized access or misuse.

<Tip>
  Tip: Always enable 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication) if available to further secure your account.
</Tip>

---

## Third-Party Services
LokaNode integrates with third-party systems such as:
- **Payment gateways** (for QRIS, credit balance, etc.)  
- **Email services** (for notifications and password resets)  
- **Analytics tools** (for performance insights)  

These partners only receive the minimum data required to perform their function and are bound by their own privacy policies.

---

## Data Retention
We retain your information only as long as necessary to:
- Maintain your account and service history  
- Comply with legal obligations  
- Resolve disputes or enforce agreements  

When you close your account, your data is **safely removed or anonymized** from our systems.

---

## Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Request access to your stored data  
- Ask for corrections or deletion of incorrect information  
- Withdraw consent for specific data usage (where applicable)  

For any privacy-related request, contact our support team.

---

## Policy Updates
This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically.  
When changes occur, we will notify users through our website or email announcement.
